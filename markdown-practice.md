##My Learning Goals
**Artificial Intelligence (AI)** is a technology that enables computers and machines to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence such as:
learning, problem-solving, understanding language, and making decisions. 
_AI is used in everyday applications_ like virtual assistants, _chatbots_, recommendation systems, and self-driving vehicles. It works by analyzing data, identifying patterns, and improving its performance over time. 
AI helps increase efficiency,automate repetitive tasks and solve complex problems across various fields including:
healthcare, education, business and agriculture.
